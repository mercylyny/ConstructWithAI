================================================================================
FUNCTIONALITY
================================================================================

1. clean_ocr_text(text: str) -> str
   ✅ Removes duplicated spaces
   ✅ Removes non-printable characters
   ✅ Normalizes line breaks (\r\n -> \n)
   ✅ Removes OCR garbage symbols (░▒▓█■□▪▫┌┐└┘├┤┬┴┼─│)
   ✅ Preserves meaningful engineering text
   ✅ Removes lines with only special characters

2. extract_measurements(text: str) -> List[str]
   ✅ Detects: 1200mm, 3.5m, 240cm
   ✅ Detects: 240x1200, 10x20 (dimensions)
   ✅ Detects: 1 200mm, 3 600 (with separators)
   ✅ Returns unique measurements (no duplicates)

3. extract_labels(text: str) -> List[str]
   ✅ Extracts uppercase words (2+ characters)
   ✅ Examples: ROOM, WALL, DOOR, WINDOW, COLUMN, GRID, STAIRS
   ✅ Filters noise words: PAGE, LOW, CONFIDENCE, OCR, FAILED
   ✅ Ignores short or meaningless tokens
   ✅ Returns unique labels
