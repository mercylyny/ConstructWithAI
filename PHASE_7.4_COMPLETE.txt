================================================================================
CALCULATION LOGIC
================================================================================

1. Wall Thickness Defaults:
   - "Partition" or "Internal" walls (INT): 0.15m
   - Load-bearing / External / Default: 0.20m

2. Formulas:
   - Area (sqm) = Length (m) × Height (m)
   - Volume (cum) = Area (sqm) × Thickness (m)

3. Totals:
   - Aggregates total_wall_area_sqm
   - Aggregates total_wall_volume_cum
